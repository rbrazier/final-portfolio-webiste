AI wingmirrors

AI wingmirrors is conceptual idea i have thought of which would improve the safety of drivers. AI wingmirrors would identify if it is clear to overtake behind you by highlighting the cars behind you in your wingmirrors. Depending on the speed of the car behind you it would go from green which is the same speed as you to to red which extremely fast. This would be extremely useful at night to help tired drivers identify cars behind them. The thickness of the car outlining can be used to signify the distance of the car beind you. Furthermore the wingmirrors could be programmed to extend and retract to cover all blind spots so when you are driving no matter where you need to look you will always be able to see. This is a project of which i will embark on most likely after my a-levels as this will be very time consuming but the theoretiocal practicality and scope of the project facsinates me.
